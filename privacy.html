Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to Zokey ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using Zokey, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Product Preferences: When you use our product recommendation features, we collect your preferences, budget ranges, and priorities to provide personalized recommendations.
  • Search Queries: Custom search terms you enter when looking for products.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Identifier: We generate and store a unique device identifier to maintain your account and preferences across sessions.
  • Region and Currency: We detect your device's locale settings to provide region-appropriate product recommendations and pricing.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, searches performed, and products viewed.

Subscription Information

If you subscribe to Zokey Premium, payment processing is handled securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized AI-powered product recommendations
  • Maintain and improve our services
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Track your remaining free searches
  • Communicate with you about updates or changes to our services
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App experience

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption.
  • Device identifiers and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Our App may use third-party services that collect information:

  • Apple App Store: For subscription management and payment processing
  • AI Services: We use artificial intelligence services to generate product recommendations. Your search queries and preferences may be processed by these services.
  • Affiliate Partners: When you click on product links, you may be directed to third-party retailer websites. These sites have their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights
