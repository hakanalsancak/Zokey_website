About Zokey

Zokey is an AI-powered iOS mobile application currently in development. The app helps users research products by guiding them through structured questions such as category, budget range, and personal preferences.

Based on this information, the app provides product recommendations and redirects users to Amazon to complete their purchase.

Zokey does not sell products directly and does not process payments.
