How it works

From idea to checkout in three steps

1

Describe what you need

Start by telling Zokey what you’re shopping for – from laptops and headphones to kitchen appliances, beauty, sports gear, baby products, books, and more.

2

Answer a few focused questions

Zokey asks short, targeted questions to understand your budget, preferences, and priorities, helping narrow down the best-fit options.

3

Review picks and buy on Amazon

The app presents curated product suggestions and then redirects you to Amazon, where you complete the purchase using your existing Amazon account.

Zokey is an independent AI-powered iOS application that focuses solely on product discovery and research. It is designed to save time and reduce decision fatigue for Amazon customers.

All orders, payments, shipping, returns, and customer service are handled entirely by Amazon. Zokey does not manage transactions, inventory, fulfillment, or customer support.

Zokey does not sell products directly and does not process payments. Users are always redirected to Amazon to review product details and place orders.
